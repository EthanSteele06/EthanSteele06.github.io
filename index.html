About Us!

Dionysia is a 4-star hotel, taking its name after the Ancient Greek festival held in praise of Dionysus, the god of wine.
We are located in Northern Bordeaux, France, the wine capital of the world. Much like the city of Bordeaux itself, we pride ourself on a long history of awards and honors.
At Dionysia, we have one primary goal: the guests' experience. We aspire to perfect a palace of pleasure and relaxation for our clients.

Hotel Housekeeper

Return to Home Page.


Job Details:


Dionysia is looking for talented employees to help us create a clean, sleek atmosphere for our clients.


Join us for the opportunity to build upon the incredible foundation laid by those in our history.


Responsibilities:

  • Perform a variety of cleaning activities such as sweeping, mopping, dusting and polishing.
  • Ensure all rooms are cared for and inspected according to standards.
  • Protect equipment and make sure there are no inadequacies.
  • Notify superiors on any damages, deficits and disturbances.
  • Deal with reasonable complaints/requests with professionalism and patience.
  • Check stocking levels of all consumables and replace when appropriate.
  • Adhere strictly to rules regarding health and safety and be aware of any company-related practices.

Qualifications:

  • Ability to work with little supervision and maintain a high level of performance.
  • Customer-oriented and friendly.
  • Prioritization and time management skills.
  • Working quickly without compromising quality.
  • Knowledge of English language.

Benefits:

  • Compensation includes base salary pay plus monthly, quarterly, and yearly bonuses.
  • Opportunities to advance within the company for qualified candidates with a dedication to service.
  • Medical, Dental and Vision Insurance.
  • Paid Parental Leave.
  • Company Paid Short Term and Long Term Disability Insurance.

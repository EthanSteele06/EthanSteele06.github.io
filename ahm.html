Assistant Hotel Manager

Return to Home Page.


Job Details:


Dionysia is looking for experienced administators with creative visions to lead our team, from the ground floor and up.


Join us for the opportunity to build upon the incredible foundation laid by those in our history.


Responsibilities:

  • Facilitate a teamwork environment and enjoy recruiting, orienting, training, and counseling a team of 5-8 staff members.
  • Partner with the General Hotel Manager to achieve a highly productive team capable of exceeding revenue expectations while also exceeding operational and guest service standards.
  • Interviewing, hiring, training, disciplinary actions, annual performance evaluations, biweekly timesheet review.
  • Monitoring and following up on guest feedback and concerns, suggest and sell amenities, ensure security needs of guests and property are met.
  • Daily bank deposits, completion of sales and marketing objectives.
  • Inventory management, relationship building with vendors and guests, teamwork.

Qualifications:

  • Excellent team building and communication abilities.
  • Strong organizational time management skills and problem-solving skills
  • Able to lift, push, and pull up to 20 lbs.
  • Excellent communication skills: oral and written.
  • Repetitive climbing of stairs, ladders.

Benefits:

  • Compensation includes base salary pay plus monthly, quarterly, and yearly bonuses.
  • Opportunities to advance within the company for qualified candidates with a dedication to service.
  • Medical, Dental and Vision Insurance.
  • Paid Parental Leave.
  • Company Paid Short Term and Long Term Disability Insurance.
